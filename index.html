Welcome

Welcome to Restaurante Peak — a sanctuary of Northern Italian culinary tradition in the heart of Enugu. Every dish is crafted from generations-old recipes, refined by Chef Marco Deluca over two decades of training across Italy's finest kitchens.

We believe food is memory, connection, and art. Step inside, and let us take you somewhere extraordinary.

Why Choose Us?

  • Authentic hand-rolled pasta crafted fresh every morning
  • Slow-cooked ragù simmered for 8 hours
  • Finest imported Italian and local Nigerian ingredients
  • Intimate dining atmosphere — only 40 covers
  • Award-winning chef with Michelin-starred pedigree
  • Zero-waste kitchen committed to seasonal sourcing
